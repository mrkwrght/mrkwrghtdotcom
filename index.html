Hello, my name is Mark Wright.

I am an interaction designer based in North East England. I work with people and clients across the UK.

I create designs based on user needs and research. I build and use prototypes to test my ideas to make sure they are usable, accessible and meet the needs of the people who use them.

I am currently designed government transactional services for HM Revenue and Customs. I have worked on services for NHS Business Services Authority.
